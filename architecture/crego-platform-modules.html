Note on Co-lending: Co-lending is an LMS product variant — adds account splitting, split ledger entries, and multi-lender approval routing within LMS. Activated via license flags on top of the LMS module.

Contact
Borrowers, guarantors, suppliers, buyers — all party management

Resources
Contacts Namespaces
Features

L
LMS (Product, Program, Loan Account, Limit, Config)
Core lending management — the heart of the platform
▶
Resources
Products Programs Accounts Limits Components Ledgers Transactions Schedules Demands Configs Dashboards
Features
  • Products — loan product definitions with types (term loan, SCF SID/PID/VF/DF)
  • Programs — loan programs with parent-child hierarchy and partner assignments
  • Accounts — loan accounts with sub-accounts, sharing (main/shared), levels, and status lifecycle
  • Limits — credit/exposure limits with availability tracking and utilization
  • Configs — overridable at product, program, and account level (interest, fees, penalties, DPD thresholds)
  • Schedules — repayment schedule generation with multiple demand components
  • Demands — payment demands with component breakdown (principal, interest, fees) and posting types (outstanding, paid, waived, refunded, excess, accrued, tax)
  • Transactions — full transaction ledger with DPD tracking, penalties, interest accrual, charges
  • Components — configurable transaction components (Principal, Interest, Processing Fee, etc.)
  • Dashboards — portfolio KPIs, product-level analytics
  • Transaction Summary — aggregated cross-account transaction views
  • Dynamic Properties — custom fields per product, program, or account
  • Co-lending — account splitting across lenders, proportional ledger entries (LMS variant, activated via license flags)

▶
Resources

  • Transaction Batches — bulk disbursement, bulk repayment, batch reconciliation
  • Excess & Refund — excess amount tracking and refund processing

  • Multi-Account — payments linked to multiple programs and accounts

General Ledger (GL)
Double-entry accounting, chart of accounts, financial periods
▶
Resources
GL Accounts GL Entries GL Mapping Posting Batches Financial Periods Journal Vouchers GL Components GL Reports
Features
  • Chart of Accounts — hierarchical GL accounts (Asset, Liability, Equity, Revenue, Expense)

  • GL Mapping — rules that map transaction types to GL accounts
  • Journal Vouchers — manual GL entries with multi-line debit/credit, approval, and reversal
  • Posting Batches — bulk GL posting with status tracking
  • Financial Periods — accounting period management (open, closed, locked)
  • Tax Master — tax codes and rates with GL account mapping
  • Trial Balance — opening/current balance tracking per GL account
  • Per-Lender Accounts — main/shared GL accounts for co-lending scenarios

Maker-Checker (Approvals & Reviews)
Multi-level approvals, approval matrices, and entity-level reviews
▶

Approvals Reviews
